# 🎬 Movie Success Analysis

## 📌 Objective

This project analyzes factors that influence a movie’s financial success using real-world data. The goal is to identify relationships between budget, revenue, ratings, and genres.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Python
* pandas
* matplotlib

---

## 📊 Key Questions

* Do higher budgets lead to higher revenue?
* Which genres generate the most revenue?
* Do higher ratings correlate with financial success?

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Budget vs Revenue

There is a strong positive relationship (correlation ≈ 0.73) between budget and revenue, indicating that higher-budget films tend to generate higher revenue. However, this relationship is not perfect, suggesting other factors also play a role.

---

### 2. Genre Performance

Animation, Adventure, and Science Fiction genres generate the highest average revenue, showing that large-scale and visually engaging films tend to perform better commercially.

In contrast, genres such as Documentary and Foreign films generate significantly lower revenue, likely due to smaller target audiences.

---

### 3. Profitability Insights

Some movies achieve high profitability despite moderate budgets, indicating that factors such as storytelling, marketing, and audience appeal can strongly influence success.

---

## 📈 Conclusion

While budget is an important factor in a movie’s success, it is not the only determinant. Genre and other external factors also play a significant role in influencing revenue outcomes.

---

TMDB 5000 Movies Dataset (Kaggle)
